## The Details
Binaural Beats is an app that allows users to relax, meditate or sleep with the use of a binaural beats generator. The users can pick a frequency and/or a background soundtrack to achieve their goals.

## The Features
* Binaural Beats player.
* Setting a timer to stop after the desired time.
* Rain sounds generation.
* Time listening the beats is logged in Apple Health.
* The app can be controlled using Siri.
