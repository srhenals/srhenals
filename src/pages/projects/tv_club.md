## The Details
TV Club is an application to allow users to keep track of their favorite shows and be notified about new episodes being released. They are able to mark watched episodes and register which shows they would like to watch.

## The Features
* iCloud sync between multiple devices.
* Today and tomorrow widgets allowing the users to quickly see upcoming episodes.
* Notifications when new episodes are about to air.
